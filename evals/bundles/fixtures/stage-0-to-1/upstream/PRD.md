# Product Requirements Document: SkillsOS

## Problem statement

Knowledge workers lose 30% of their day searching for institutional knowledge scattered
across Slack, Notion, and email threads. SkillsOS provides a single, agent-augmented
workspace that surfaces the right context at the right moment.

## Target users

- Individual contributors at 50-500 person companies
- Team leads who need to onboard new hires faster
- Knowledge managers maintaining internal wikis

## Jobs to be done

1. "When I start a task, I need the relevant context surfaced automatically so I don't
   spend 20 minutes searching Slack."
2. "When a new team member joins, I need our shared mental models captured and
   transferable without a 1:1 session."

## Out of scope

- Consumer market
- Sub-10-person startups
- Real-time collaboration features (Phase 2)

## Success metrics

- Search-to-answer time < 2 minutes (p75)
- New hire time-to-productivity -40% vs baseline
- Daily active usage > 60% of seats after 90 days
