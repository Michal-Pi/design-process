# Research Assumptions

## Validated Assumptions

1. **Target user is a mid-market PM (10-50 person team)** — confirmed via screener in all 5 interviews.
2. **Primary pain is tool fragmentation, not task management per se** — confirmed via JTBD analysis.
3. **Mobile usage is secondary** — 4/5 interviewees said they use the tool on desktop 90%+ of the time.

## Proto-Grade Assumptions (not yet validated)

1. **Enterprise buyers need SSO** — assumed from market research, not direct interviews.
2. **Notification fatigue is the top frustration** — inferred from interview tone, not directly measured.

## Out-of-scope Assumptions

- We are NOT assuming a specific tech stack for the product.
- We are NOT validating pricing model assumptions in Stage 1.
