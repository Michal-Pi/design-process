# Wayfind — Travel Itinerary Planner

## Problem
Travelers spend hours across 5–10 tabs assembling itineraries. Bookings, maps, and notes are never in one place.

## Target Users
- Independent travelers planning trips of 5–14 days
- Couples and small groups (2–5 people) planning together

## Jobs-to-be-Done
1. When I'm planning a trip, I want to drag flights, hotels, and activities onto a timeline and see the gaps.
2. When I travel, I want my itinerary offline on my phone with map pins for every stop.
3. When something changes (flight delay, canceled reservation), I want the itinerary to update and notify my travel companions.

## Scope (v1)
- Drag-and-drop day-by-day itinerary editor
- Import from email (flight + hotel confirmations)
- Offline mode (PWA)
- Shared itinerary with real-time sync (up to 5 collaborators)
- Embedded map view

## Out of Scope
- Booking/purchasing within the app
- Expense splitting
- Visa/entry requirement checks
