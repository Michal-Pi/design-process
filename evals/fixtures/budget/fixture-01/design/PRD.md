# TaskFlow — Task Management App

## Problem
Teams lose track of tasks across tools. TaskFlow is a unified task manager.

## Target Users
- Knowledge workers at SMBs (5–50 employees)
- Project managers

## Jobs-to-be-Done
1. When I start my day, I want to see what needs my attention today.
2. When a deadline moves, I want to update and notify my team immediately.

## Scope (v1)
- Task creation, assignment, due dates
- Kanban and list views
- Basic notifications

## Out of Scope
- Time tracking
- Billing
