# Ledge — Personal Finance Tracker

## Problem
Most personal finance apps either require bank integration (privacy concern) or are too manual to maintain.

## Target Users
- Adults 25–45 who want to track spending without linking bank accounts
- Couples managing a shared budget

## Jobs-to-be-Done
1. When I make a purchase, I want to log it in under 10 seconds.
2. At month end, I want to see where I overspent compared to my category budgets.
3. When my partner and I disagree on spending, I want to show a neutral, shared view.

## Scope (v1)
- Manual transaction entry (receipt scan via camera)
- Category budgets with progress bars
- Shared household view (two-person)
- Monthly reports

## Out of Scope
- Bank/credit card sync
- Investment tracking
- Tax optimization
