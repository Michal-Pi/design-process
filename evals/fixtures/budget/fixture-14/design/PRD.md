# Bloom — Plant Care Companion

## Problem
Plant owners kill their plants because they forget to water or don't know why leaves are yellowing.

## Target Users
- Urban apartment dwellers with 3–20 houseplants
- Beginners who want guidance, not just reminders

## Jobs-to-be-Done
1. When I buy a new plant, I want to identify it by photo and immediately get its care schedule.
2. When a leaf starts looking odd, I want to photograph it and get a likely diagnosis with a treatment plan.
3. When I go on vacation, I want to set a "vacation mode" that adjusts care reminders for a house-sitter.

## Scope (v1)
- Plant identification by photo (ML model)
- Personalized watering, fertilizing, repotting reminders
- Leaf symptom checker (photo → diagnosis)
- Plant care journal (photo log + notes)
- Vacation mode with sitter-friendly checklist

## Out of Scope
- Community plant swaps
- Smart sensor integration (soil moisture)
- Plant shop marketplace
