# Venue — Event Space Discovery Platform

## Problem
Event planners waste days contacting venues that are already booked, too small, or outside budget. There's no Airbnb for event spaces.

## Target Users
- Corporate event planners (offsites, team dinners, client events, 20–200 attendees)
- Individual event organizers (weddings, birthday parties, workshops)

## Jobs-to-be-Done
1. When I need a venue for a 50-person offsite in 3 weeks, I want to see available spaces within my budget, filter by AV/catering requirements, and request holds on 3 options in one session.
2. When a venue is tentatively booked, I want to e-sign the contract and pay a deposit without leaving the platform.
3. When I'm comparing 3 venues, I want a side-by-side spec sheet with photos, pricing, and availability calendar.

## Scope (v1)
- Venue listing with photos, capacity, amenities, pricing
- Real-time availability calendar
- Instant booking and tentative hold requests
- E-signature + deposit collection (Stripe)
- Side-by-side venue comparison view
- Venue dashboard for space owners (listing management, booking requests)

## Out of Scope
- Catering/vendor marketplace
- Guest management and invitations
- Post-event feedback and reviews (v2)
