# Gloss — Terminology & Jargon Explainer

## Problem
Teams accumulate internal jargon, acronyms, and domain terms. New hires and cross-functional collaborators constantly interrupt experts to ask "what does that mean?"

## Target Users
- Fast-growing startups (20–200 employees) with strong internal vocabulary
- Enterprise teams onboarding new hires or cross-team collaborators

## Jobs-to-be-Done
1. When I encounter an unfamiliar term in a doc, I want an inline definition without leaving my workflow.
2. When I write a doc, I want to flag terms that newcomers might not know.
3. When the team's vocabulary drifts, I want a glossary that stays current through crowdsourced edits.

## Scope (v1)
- Browser extension: highlight and define terms on any web page
- Notion + Confluence + Slack app integrations
- Crowdsourced glossary editor (propose, vote, approve terms)
- AI-assisted term detection in documents

## Out of Scope
- Multi-language support
- Enterprise SSO (v2)
- Analytics on term usage frequency
