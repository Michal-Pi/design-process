# MealKit — Recipe & Ingredient Planner

## Problem
Home cooks struggle to plan weekly meals and avoid food waste.

## Target Users
- Families with 2–4 members cooking at home 4+ nights/week
- Health-conscious individuals

## Jobs-to-be-Done
1. When planning the week, I want to pick recipes and auto-generate a shopping list.
2. When I have leftover ingredients, I want recipe suggestions that use them.

## Scope (v1)
- Recipe browsing and saving
- Weekly meal planner calendar
- Auto-generated shopping list
- Ingredient inventory tracker

## Out of Scope
- Grocery delivery integration
- Nutritional analysis
