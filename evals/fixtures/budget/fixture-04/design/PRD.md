# FocusBlock — Deep Work Scheduler

## Problem
Knowledge workers struggle to protect large blocks of time for deep work. Meetings fragment calendars.

## Target Users
- Individual contributors and managers who do creative or analytical work
- Remote teams on Google Workspace or Microsoft 365

## Jobs-to-be-Done
1. When I start my week, I want to auto-schedule 3+ hour deep work blocks around existing meetings.
2. When someone tries to book a meeting during my focus block, I want them to see an alternative slot.

## Scope (v1)
- Calendar sync (Google Calendar, Outlook)
- AI-suggested focus block scheduling
- "Focus shield" that blocks booking during protected slots
- Daily focus report

## Out of Scope
- Mobile app (web only in v1)
- Team focus analytics
