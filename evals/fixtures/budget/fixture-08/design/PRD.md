# Podlog — Podcast Listening Tracker

## Problem
Podcast listeners have no way to track what they've listened to, take timestamped notes, or discover episodes based on their listening history.

## Target Users
- Avid podcast listeners (6+ hours/week)
- Journalists and researchers who use podcasts as a source

## Jobs-to-be-Done
1. When I finish an episode, I want to log key takeaways without leaving the app.
2. When I recommend an episode to a friend, I want to share a link with my personal notes visible.
3. When I'm looking for my next episode, I want recommendations based on topics I've engaged with.

## Scope (v1)
- Podcast search and subscribe (via RSS)
- Per-episode listening log with timestamped notes
- Public/private note sharing
- Basic discovery feed (topic tags)

## Out of Scope
- In-app audio playback
- Host/guest profile pages
- Podcast analytics for creators
