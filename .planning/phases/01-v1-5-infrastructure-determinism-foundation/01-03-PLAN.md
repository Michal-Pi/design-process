Without per-skill skillgrade + aggregate coexistence, trigger discipline is "ship and pray" and the Codex 2% cap (Pitfall 9) bites at GA. This plan also documents the TRIG-04 contingency (split into core + atoms) — it does not execute the split; that lever fires only if empirics show aggregate <0.80.

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-03-01 | Tampering | `evals/fixtures/golden/<script>/expected.*` | mitigate | Regen requires explicit `npm run regen-golden -- --script <name> --reason "<text>"` per D-14; reason committed alongside diff in PR |
| T-03-02 | Repudiation | Aggregate-coexistence CI is not blocking | accept | Open Q3 explicitly calibrates the threshold during Phase 1; CI reports the number; v2.0 GA enables blocking |
| T-03-03 | Tampering | Lint-determinism regex could be subverted by string-concat-style imports (`import("anth" + "ropic")`) | accept | Phase 1's regex catches the 99% case; dynamic-import imports are flagged by ESLint `no-restricted-imports` (defense in depth) — anyone going out of their way to bypass both layers is acting in bad faith |
| T-03-04 | Information Disclosure | A real `CLAUDE_CODE_BIN` dispatch could leak fixture PII via prompts | accept | Phase 1 fixtures contain no PII (verified by Plan 04 PII scanner once it lands); coexistence triggers are public-domain prompts |
